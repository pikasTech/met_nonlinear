\begin{abstract}
    \CNEN{
        本文针对电化学地震检波器（MET）振幅相关的频率响应非线性问题，提出了一种 novel 的前馈式补偿网络(FRIKAN)，结合了频响先验注入 and 物理约束 KAN。前馈架构避免了力反馈的稳定性风险，同时降低了系统复杂度，将待补偿系统与理想系统的相对频率响应作为物理先验信息注入 RNN，并利用了 KAN 中的可训练激活函数对传感器输入-输出曲线的天然结构适配性提高单个神经元的非线性补偿能力，同时引入奇对称性和同向性物理约束克服传统神经网络因物理信息缺失导致的过拟合与性能退化瓶颈，实现 MET 非线性补偿。基于振动激励实验首次系统性地构建包含幅频耦合效应的三维频率响应数据集，并设计了适配数据集的幅频损失函数，提高了频域拟合精度。测试结果表明，FRIKAN 在振幅增大超过10倍的情况下，将MET的输入-输出非线性平均降低了\valNonlinearSuppression，灵敏度漂移减小\valSensitivityDriftSuppression，固有频率漂移减小\valFnDriftSuppression，与LSTM、GRU、RVTDCNN 典型模型相比，在参数量相近时，FRIKAN 的补偿效果平均提升了\valCompImprove。此外，提出了查找表加速方案实现该方法的嵌入式部署，计算效率较LSTM提高\valSpeedup 倍(精度损失小于 0.5\%)，具有promising 的应用前景。}{This paper addresses the magnitude-dependent nonlinearity in the frequency response of electrochemical seismometers (METs) by proposing a novel feedforward compensation network that integrates frequency response prior injection and physics-constrained KAN (FRIKAN). The feedforward architecture avoids stability risks associated with force feedback while reducing system complexity. It injects the relative frequency response between the target system and an ideal system as physical prior into the RNN, leveraging the trainable activation function in KAN to enhance individual neurons' nonlinear compensation capability through natural structural adaptation to sensor input-output curves. Physical constraints of odd symmetry and positivity are introduced to overcome overfitting and performance degradation caused by lack of physical information in traditional neural networks, achieving MET nonlinear compensation. For the first time, a systematic 3D frequency response dataset incorporating amplitude-frequency coupling effects is constructed through vibration excitation experiments. A tailored amplitude-frequency loss function is designed to improve frequency-domain fitting accuracy. Test results show that FRIKAN reduces MET's input-output nonlinearity by \valNonlinearSuppression on average with magnitude increases over 10×, while decreasing sensitivity drift by \valSensitivityDriftSuppression and natural frequency drift by \valFnDriftSuppression. Compared to LSTM, GRU, and RVTDCNN with similar parameter counts, FRIKAN improves compensation performance by \valCompImprove on average. A lookup table acceleration strategy enables embedded deployment, achieving \valSpeedup× computational efficiency gain over LSTM (accuracy loss <0.5\%), showing promising application potential.%<CNHash>C34F7352</CNHash>
}

\end{abstract}

\section{Introduction}

\CNEN{
    电化学地震检波器（MET）因其高灵敏度与宽频带、抗倾斜、抗冲击等特点，在地下资源勘探、地震监测领域展现出潜力，有望成为下一代地震检波器的主导力量\cite{10841463}。随着全球地震观测网络（GSNs）逐步实现宽频带、高动态范围的实时数据采集\cite{WOS:000853457100001}，对核心传感器的线性度与稳定性提出了更严苛的要求，然而，电化学反应与流体力学耦合的复杂过程导致了 MET 频率响应在不同振幅下出现显著非线性，包括灵敏度和固有频率的漂移，从而造成震动波形检测失真，严重限制了动态范围，影响地震勘探数据质量与地震事件可靠检测。因而，如何改进非线性频率响应成为当前研究的热点。
}{
Electrochemical seismometers (METs) exhibit potential in underground resource exploration and seismic monitoring due to their high sensitivity, wide bandwidth, and tilt/impact resistance, positioning them as leading candidates for next-generation seismometers\cite{10841463}. With global seismic networks (GSNs) progressively achieving real-time data acquisition with broadband and high dynamic range\cite{WOS:000853457100001}, stricter demands are placed on core sensors' linearity and stability. However, the complex coupling between electrochemical reactions and fluid mechanics introduces significant magnitude-dependent nonlinearity in MET frequency response, including sensitivity and natural frequency drift. This distorts vibration waveform detection, severely limiting dynamic range and compromising seismic exploration data quality and reliable event detection. Consequently, improving nonlinear frequency response has become a research priority.
%<CNHash>D5BDCC0A</CNHash>
}

\CNEN{
    已有研究尝试从结构设计的改善提升线性度。Xu 等\cite{9481294}等提出了一种基于阳极键合技术的MEMS电化学地震传感器，通过硅-玻璃-硅三层集成电极结构提高了灵敏度和器件间一致性，但受 MET 复杂的电化学-机电耦合机制的限制，结构优化手段对线性度的提升效果有限且难以突破固有物理限制，系统仅在振幅低于 1.0 mm/s@20Hz（ 0.13 $\text{m}/\text{s}^2$）时才能保持线性工作特性。力反馈方式能够有效抑制非线性，例如，Li 等\cite{li_electrochemical_2017}在振幅小于 1.8 mm/s，分别在 1Hz (0.011$\text{m}/\text{s}^2$) 和 5Hz (0.057 $\text{m}/\text{s}^2$)下测试了 MET 的非线性表现，并通过力反馈将平均灵敏度漂移降低了 \valSensitivityDriftSuppressionLI，Sun 等\cite{WOS:000408118700046}在振幅小于 3.7 mm/s@20Hz（0.46 $\text{m}/\text{s}^2$）的条件下测试了 MET，并使用力反馈将输入输出非线性误差降低了 \valNonlinearSuppressionSUN。然而力反馈需依赖额外机械反馈单元实时产生反馈力，增加了系统复杂度和制造成本，更严重的是，大振幅下非线性带来的稳定裕度下降和闭环振荡风险严重制约系统稳定性，限制其在更宽频带和更大动态范围的实用性，由此可见亟需不依赖硬件反馈单元的补偿方法。在数字预失真方法的启发\cite{wangInputAmplitudeBasedAdaptive2024}下，本研究提出了一种用于 MET 的前馈补偿架构。相比于力反馈系统，前馈补偿架构具有结构稳定性优势，无需考虑闭环稳定裕度问题，便于在大动态范围下保持系统可靠性。此外，前馈架构通过软件算法实现补偿，可以大幅降低硬件复杂度，更适合便携式和野外应用场景。
}{
    Previous studies have attempted to improve linearity through structural design optimization. Xu et al. \cite{9481294} proposed a MEMS electrochemical seismometer using anodic bonding technology, which enhanced sensitivity and device consistency via a silicon-glass-silicon three-layer integrated electrode structure. However, constrained by the complex electrochemical-mechanical coupling mechanism of MET, structural optimization showed limited effectiveness in linearity improvement and could not overcome inherent physical limitations. The system maintained linear operation only when the magnitude was below 1.0 mm/s@20Hz (0.13 $\text{m}/\text{s}^2$). Force feedback effectively suppresses nonlinearity. For instance, Li et al. \cite{li_electrochemical_2017} tested MET's nonlinear performance at magnitudes below 1.8 mm/s under 1Hz (0.011$\text{m}/\text{s}^2$) and 5Hz (0.057 $\text{m}/\text{s}^2$), reducing average sensitivity drift by \valSensitivityDriftSuppressionLI via force feedback. Sun et al. \cite{WOS:000408118700046} tested MET at magnitudes below 3.7 mm/s@20Hz (0.46 $\text{m}/\text{s}^2$) and reduced input-output nonlinear error by \valNonlinearSuppressionSUN using force feedback. However, force feedback relies on additional mechanical feedback units to generate real-time feedback force, increasing system complexity and manufacturing costs. More critically, nonlinearity-induced stability margin reduction and closed-loop oscillation risks at large magnitudes severely constrain system stability, limiting its practicality in wider bandwidth and larger dynamic range applications. This highlights the urgent need for compensation methods independent of hardware feedback units. Inspired by digital predistortion techniques \cite{wangInputAmplitudeBasedAdaptive2024}, this study proposes a feedforward compensation framework for MET. Compared to force feedback systems, the feedforward framework offers inherent stability advantages by eliminating closed-loop phase margin concerns, ensuring reliability in large dynamic range scenarios. Moreover, its software-based implementation significantly reduces hardware complexity, making it more suitable for portable and field applications.
%<CNHash>E7CC54C5</CNHash>
}
\CNEN{
    前馈补偿方法需要充足、完备的时域-频域测试数据，然而已有研究局限于有限的频率、振幅条件。本研究实施了覆盖全工作频带的系统性测试方案，将测试频率上限由 20Hz 扩展至 128Hz，振幅上限由 0.46 $\text{m}/\text{s}^2$ 扩展至 6.0 $\text{m}/\text{s}^2$，首次构建了包含幅频耦合效应的 三维频率响应数据集(包括振幅，频率，输入输出时间序列) 。
}{
Feedforward compensation methods require comprehensive time-frequency domain test data, yet existing studies are limited to restricted frequency and magnitude conditions. This study implements a systematic testing scheme covering the full bandwidth, extending the upper test frequency from 20Hz to 128Hz and the upper magnitude from 0.46 $\text{m}/\text{s}^2$ to 6.0 $\text{m}/\text{s}^2$. For the first time, a three-dimensional frequency response dataset (including magnitude, frequency, and input-output time series) incorporating amplitude-frequency coupling effects is constructed.
%<CNHash>976E9CBF</CNHash>
}

\CNEN{
    在完备测试数据的基础上，如何利用这些数据开发高效补偿算法成为新的核心挑战。现有信号处理领域提出了多种传统方法，如摄动法\cite{royOptimalModifiedHomotopy2023}、多尺度法\cite{jainModelOrderReduction2020}、调和平衡法\cite{shawHybridAveragingHarmonic2023}、以及基于 Volterra 级数\cite{helieInputOutputReduced2021}、Wiener 等模型\cite{ sersourNonlinearSystemIdentification2018}的识别方法。然而，这些模型在处理类似 MET 这种电化学-流体-机械强耦合系统时普遍受限，存在高阶计算量庞大和模型参数难以精确确定的问题。
}{
    With comprehensive test data available, developing efficient compensation algorithms becomes the new core challenge. The signal processing field has proposed various conventional methods, such as the perturbation method \cite{royOptimalModifiedHomotopy2023}, multi-scale method \cite{jainModelOrderReduction2020}, harmonic balance \cite{shawHybridAveragingHarmonic2023}, and identification approaches based on Volterra series \cite{helieInputOutputReduced2021} and Wiener models \cite{sersourNonlinearSystemIdentification2018}. However, these models face limitations when handling strongly coupled electrochemical-fluid-mechanical systems like MET, exhibiting high computational complexity and difficulties in precisely determining model parameters.
%<CNHash>1908C407</CNHash>
}

\CNEN{
    数据驱动的深度学习方法因其端到端的优越学习能力逐渐受到关注，LSTM/GRU 等深度学习方法在非线性系统输出预测方面表现出色，但通常被视为“黑盒”，且在实时信号处理中面临过拟合与计算量大的问题\cite{zhaoDetectingEarlyDamages2020}。为弥合物理与数据驱动的差距，一些研究开始将物理信息融入神经网络（如 PINNs\cite{wangEigenvectorBiasFourier2021}），但 MET 难以由偏微分方程精确描述，这使得基于微分方程约束的物理信息神经网络难以直接应用。除了偏微分精确描述外，亦有物理启发式研究试图将传感器环境参数与网络结构结合\cite{motaqiArtificialIntelligenceBasedPowerTemperature2022}，在功率放大器领域取得良好效果，但尚未应用于传感器非线性补偿。频率响应信息是频率响应漂移补偿任务具有针对性的关键物理信息，为了将频率响应信息融入神经网络，本研究提出了可显式控制频率响应的多通道 RNN 线性核阵列(FRIRNN)，以表征在不同振幅下 MET 的频率响应特征，每个 RNN 线性核的阶次仅有 4 个隐状态，显著降低了网络的计算复杂度，为后续嵌入式硬件部署的计算量压缩奠定基础。
}{
    Data-driven deep learning methods have gained increasing attention due to their superior end-to-end learning capabilities. While LSTM/GRU-based approaches excel in nonlinear system output prediction\cite{zhaoDetectingEarlyDamages2020}, they are often treated as "black box" solutions and face challenges of overfitting and high computational cost in real-time signal processing. To bridge the gap between physics and data-driven approaches, some studies integrate physical information into neural networks (e.g., PINNs\cite{wangEigenvectorBiasFourier2021}). However, METs cannot be precisely described by PDEs, making physics-constrained networks based on differential equations difficult to apply. Beyond PDE-based descriptions, physics-inspired approaches combining sensor environmental parameters with network architectures\cite{motaqiArtificialIntelligenceBasedPowerTemperature2022} have shown promise in power amplifier linearization but remain unexplored for sensor nonlinear compensation. Frequency response information serves as the key physics prior for targeted compensation of frequency drift. To incorporate this prior, we propose a multi-channel RNN kernel array (FRIRNN) with explicit frequency response control, characterizing MET's magnitude-dependent frequency response characteristics. Each RNN kernel contains only 4 hidden states, significantly reducing computational complexity and facilitating subsequent deployment on embedded platforms.
%<CNHash>43BFE2D8</CNHash>
}

\CNEN{
    除物理先验融合外，网络结构本身的数学可解释性改进也被证明能增强模型泛化能力，传统的 MLP （多层感知机）只能通过线性权重和固定激活函数的多层加权和隐式逼近传感器的输入-输出关系，而近年来出现的 Kolmogorov-Arnold 网络（KAN）因激活函数可学习性天然适配传感器的输入-输出关系，并有望改善网络的泛化能力和提高可解释性 \cite{schmidt-hieberKolmogorovArnoldRepresentation2021, livierisCKANNewApproach2024}。}{
    Beyond physics-constrained fusion, improving the mathematical interpretability of network architecture has been proven to enhance model generalization. Traditional MLPs (multilayer perceptrons) can only implicitly approximate sensor input-output relationships through linear weights and fixed activation functions. In contrast, the recently proposed Kolmogorov-Arnold network (KAN) naturally adapts to sensor input-output mappings via learnable activation functions, showing potential to improve both generalization and interpretability \cite{schmidt-hieberKolmogorovArnoldRepresentation2021, livierisCKANNewApproach2024}.
%<CNHash>8AE4D830</CNHash>
}

\CNEN{
    综上，针对MET非线性补偿中的物理泛化能力、可解释性和计算效率难题，本研究结合 MET 的频域物理特性和 KAN 的结构可解释性优势，提出了一种结合了频响先验注入线性 RNN 和物理约束 KAN 的新型网络 FRIKAN，实现了物理信息启发和网络结构增强的双重改进。FRIKAN 网络被后置于传感器，摆脱了对高阶核或外部机械反馈的依赖。在训练时，基于包含幅频耦合效应的三维频率响应数据集设计了幅频损失函数以提高频率域敏感性。最后，为验证在资源受限平台的可用性，本文还提出基于查找表（LUT）的 KAN 网络加速方案，并在 STM32F405 上验证其实时可行性。文章的主要贡献如下：}{
In summary, to address the challenges of physical generalization, interpretability, and computational efficiency in MET nonlinear compensation, this study combines MET's frequency-domain physical characteristics with KAN's structural interpretability advantages, proposing a novel FRIKAN network integrating frequency response prior-injected linear RNN and physics-constrained KAN. This achieves dual improvements in physics-informed inspiration and network architecture enhancement. The FRIKAN network is post-positioned to the sensor, eliminating dependence on high-order kernels or external mechanical feedback. During training, an amplitude-frequency loss function is designed based on a 3D frequency response dataset incorporating amplitude-frequency coupling effects to enhance frequency-domain sensitivity. Finally, to validate platform feasibility, a lookup table (LUT)-based KAN acceleration scheme is proposed and verified on STM32F405 for real-time feasibility. The main contributions are as follows:

}

\subsection{Amplitude-Frequency Response MAE Loss Function}

\CNEN{传统的平均绝对误差（MAE）与均方误差（MSE）在回归任务中应用广泛，但它们主要度量点对点的误差累积，对高频随机噪声通常过度敏感，在非线性系统建模中，更关注信号整体频率特性。因此，我们设计了幅频平均绝对误差（AFMAE），以在频域层面度量补偿输出与理想输出的差异。}{Conventional mean absolute error (MAE) and mean squared error (MSE) are widely adopted in regression tasks, but they primarily measure point-to-point error accumulation and often exhibit excessive sensitivity to high-frequency random noise. For nonlinear system modeling, greater emphasis is placed on the overall frequency response of signals. Therefore, we design amplitude-frequency mean absolute error (AFMAE) to quantify the discrepancy between compensated output and ideal output in the frequency domain.%<CNHash>13CC4EF6</CNHash>
}

    \CNEN{
          为了推导 AFMAE的表达式，先考察正弦波信号的能量—幅值关系。对于周期为 $T = \frac{2\pi}{\omega}$ 的正弦波 $A\sin(\omega t)$，其能量 $E$ 可表示为公式 (\ref{eq:ContinuousEnergy})。
          }{
          To derive the expression of AFMAE, we first examine the energy-amplitude relationship of sinusoidal signals. For a sine wave $A\sin(\omega t)$ with period $T = \frac{2\pi}{\omega}$, its energy $E$ can be expressed as Eq.~(\ref{eq:ContinuousEnergy}).
%<CNHash>86D42B4A</CNHash>
}

          \begin{equation}
              E = \int_{0}^{T} A^2 \sin^2(\omega t) \, dt = \frac{1}{2} A^2 \, T
              \label{eq:ContinuousEnergy}
          \end{equation}

          \CNEN{
          在离散情况下（采样频率为 $f_s$，采样点数为 $\pmax$，则 $T = \frac{N}{f_s}$），信号能量可近似为公式 (\ref{eq:DiscreteEnergy})。
          }{
          In the discrete case (sampling frequency $f_s$, number of samples $\pmax$, thus $T = \frac{N}{f_s}$), the signal energy can be approximated by Eq.~(\ref{eq:DiscreteEnergy}).
%<CNHash>4A863DE8</CNHash>
}

          \begin{equation}
              E \approx \frac{1}{f_s} \sum_{\pidx=0}^{\pmax-1} \bigl(y[n]\bigr)^2
              \label{eq:DiscreteEnergy}
          \end{equation}

          \CNEN{
          据此，输出信号的幅值可写为 $A \approx \sqrt{\frac{2E}{T}}$。对于输入振幅为 $\magn_\midx$ 的正弦信号在频率 $f_\fidx$ 下的输出，定义其幅频响应为公式 (\ref{eq:HatH})。
          }{Accordingly, the amplitude of the output signal can be written as $A \approx \sqrt{\frac{2E}{T}}$. For a sinusoidal input with amplitude $\magn_\midx$ at frequency $f_\fidx$, its frequency response is defined by Eq.~(\ref{eq:HatH}).%<CNHash>36B61396</CNHash>
}

          \begin{equation}
              \begin{aligned}
                  \left|\pred{H}(f_\fidx,\magn_\midx)\right|
                   & = \frac{\pred{A}(f_\fidx,\magn_\midx)}{\magn_\midx}
                  = \sqrt{\frac{2}{T}} \frac{\sqrt{\hat{E}(f_\fidx,\magn_\midx)}}{\magn_\midx} \\
                   & = \sqrt{\frac{2}{T f_s}}
                  \frac{\sqrt{\sum_{\pidx=0}^{\pmax-1} \hat{y}_{\fidx,\midx}[n]^2}}{\magn_\midx}
              \end{aligned}
              \label{eq:HatH}
          \end{equation}

        \CNEN{
          其中$\pred{H}$ 为网络预测的频率响应，$\pred{A}$ 与 $\hat{E}$ 分别是网络预测得到的输出幅值与离散能量估计，$\hat{y}_{\fidx,\midx}[n]$ 为在输入振幅 $\magn_\midx$ 与频率 $f_\fidx$ 下得到的网络预测采样。
        }{
where $\pred{H}$ is the network-predicted frequency response, $\pred{A}$ and $\hat{E}$ are the network-predicted output amplitude and discrete energy estimate, respectively, and $\hat{y}_{\fidx,\midx}[n]$ is the network-predicted sample under input amplitude $\magn_\midx$ and frequency $f_\fidx$.
%<CNHash>FB3D81AA</CNHash>
}

        \CNEN{
          为了量化模型预测输出与真实输出的频率响应差异，对真实的频率响应 $H(f_\fidx,\magn_\midx)$ 与网络预测的频率响应 $\pred{H}(f_\fidx,\magn_\midx)$ 取对数做差，得到公式 (\ref{eq:LogH})。
        }{To quantify the frequency response difference between the model prediction and the true output, the logarithmic difference between the true frequency response $H(f_\fidx,\magn_\midx)$ and the network-predicted frequency response $\pred{H}(f_\fidx,\magn_\midx)$ is calculated, yielding Eq.~(\ref{eq:LogH}).%<CNHash>453D1577</CNHash>
}


          \begin{equation}
              \begin{aligned}
                    & \log  \left|H(f_\fidx,\magn_\midx)\right|  - \log\left|\pred{H}(f_\fidx, \magn_\midx)\right|                                            \\
                  = & \log \sqrt{\frac{\sum_{\pidx=0}^{\pmax-1}y_{\fidx,\midx}[n]^2}{{\sum_{\pidx=0}^{\pmax-1}\hat{y}_{\fidx,\midx}[n]^2}}}                   \\
                  = & \frac{1}{2} \left( \log \sum_{\pidx=0}^{\pmax-1}y_{\fidx,\midx}[n]^2 -  \log \sum_{\pidx=0}^{\pmax-1}\hat{y}_{\fidx,\midx}[n]^2 \right)
              \end{aligned}
              \label{eq:LogH}
          \end{equation}

          \CNEN{
          其中，$H$ 为真实的频率响应，取对数可视为在对数幅度轴（dB 标度）上度量输出差异，从而保证幅值偏差在高于真实值与低于真实值时拥有对称性，避免预测值大于真实值和预测值小于真实值在损失上的不均衡，同时抵消了对 $\magn_\midx$ 的计算需求。
          }{
          where $H$ denotes the true frequency response. Taking the logarithm ensures symmetric measurement of output differences on the logarithmic amplitude axis (dB scale), thereby balancing the loss when the predicted value exceeds or falls below the true value, while eliminating the need for $\magn_\midx$ computation.
%<CNHash>9488EBCC</CNHash>
}

          \CNEN{
          在式(\ref{eq:LogH})基础上我们简化了系数，随后定义了 $\mathcal{L}_{\text{AFMAE}}$ 为公式 (\ref{eq:AFMAE_loss2})。
          }{
          Based on Eq.~(\ref{eq:LogH}), we simplified the coefficients and subsequently defined $\mathcal{L}_{\text{AFMAE}}$ as Eq.~(\ref{eq:AFMAE_loss2}).
%<CNHash>7435E9AE</CNHash>
}

          \begin{equation}
              \begin{aligned}
                  \mathcal{L}_{\text{AFMAE}} & =  \frac{1}{\fmax\mmax} \sum_{\fidx=1}^{\fmax} \sum_{\midx=1}^{\mmax}
                  \left|
                  \log \sum_{\pidx=0}^{\pmax-1}y_{\fidx,\midx}[n]^2
                  - \log \sum_{\pidx=0}^{\pmax-1}\hat{y}_{\fidx,\midx}[n]^2

              \end{aligned}
              \label{eq:AFMAE_loss2}
          \end{equation}


\CNEN{其中$\fmax$是参与计算的频率的样本数量，$\mmax$是参与计算的振幅的样本数量。这种计算方法只需做一次能量计算，高效反映系统的幅频特性。}{where $\fmax$ is the sample count of frequencies involved in computation, and $\mmax$ is the sample count of magnitudes. This method requires only a single energy calculation, efficiently characterizing the amplitude-frequency characteristics of the system.%<CNHash>BCFD3559</CNHash>
}
\CNEN{
    在实际训练中 MAE 和 AFMAE 加权组合取得了较好的效果，我们在总体损失中添加一个加权超参数 $\wMAE$，如公式(\ref{eq:TotalLoss})所示。}{

\begin{equation}

\section{Conclusion}

\CNEN{本文通过融合频率响应物理先验与Kolmogorov-Arnold网络的结构优势，提出了FRIKAN非线性补偿框架，实现了三方面贡献：1) 物理-数据融合方面，将相对频率响应切片转化为RNN初始权重，搭建了频域专家知识与神经网络的桥梁；2) 网络结构方面，采用物理约束的可训练样条激活函数，直接学习传感器的校准曲线，使单个神经元具备更强的非线性建模能力；3) 损失函数设计方面，提出的 AFMAE 利用能量高效计算幅频差异，克服了传统MAE对高频噪声的过度敏感，实现频域特性的精确补偿}{This paper proposes the FRIKAN nonlinear compensation framework by integrating frequency response physical priors with the structural advantages of Kolmogorov-Arnold networks, achieving three contributions: 1) In physics-data fusion, slicing relative frequency responses into RNN initial weights bridges expert knowledge and neural networks in the frequency domain; 2) Architecturally, physics-constrained trainable spline activation functions directly learn sensor calibration curves, enhancing individual neurons' nonlinear modeling capability; 3) For loss function design, the proposed AFMAE efficiently computes amplitude-frequency differences using energy metrics, overcoming traditional MAE's oversensitivity to high-frequency noise and enabling precise frequency-domain compensation.%<CNHash>92F84880</CNHash>
}

\CNEN{实验结果表明，FRIKAN 模型在电化学检波器输入-输出曲线提升和频率响应漂移抑制方面优于传统方法，可以大幅改善电化学的非线性问题，推动电化学检波器在油气资源增储领域的规模化应用。同时，FRIKAN 具有低算力需求的优势，有望在嵌入式系统和大规模传感器设备中应用。}{Experimental results demonstrate that the FRIKAN model outperforms conventional methods in improving the input-output curve of electrochemical seismometers and suppressing frequency drift, significantly mitigating nonlinearity issues in electrochemical systems. This advancement promotes large-scale applications of electrochemical seismometers in oil/gas reservoir enhancement. Moreover, FRIKAN's low-compute requirement makes it promising for deployment on embedded platforms and large-scale sensor arrays.%<CNHash>1EE2B856</CNHash>
}

\CNEN{下一步的研究将会把FRIKAN集成到传感器内部，并开展大规模的多传感器野外实验，验证并进一步提升FRIKAN在地震成像中的非线性补偿效果。此外，环境因素（温度、湿度）也可能影响 MET 的非线性，多环境因素对FRIKAN补偿效果的影响以及对应的自适应补偿方法是未来的重要研究方向。
}{Future research will integrate FRIKAN into the sensor interior and conduct large-scale multi-sensor field experiments to validate and further improve its nonlinear compensation performance in seismic imaging. Additionally, environmental factors (temperature, humidity) may affect the MET's nonlinearity. Investigating their impacts on FRIKAN's compensation efficacy and developing corresponding adaptive compensation methods remain critical future directions.%<CNHash>D833D57B</CNHash>
